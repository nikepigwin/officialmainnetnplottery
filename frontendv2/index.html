About Cardano Lottery

The Cardano Lottery is a fully decentralized lottery platform built on the Cardano blockchain. Leveraging an off-chain mechanism, it automates reward distribution and employs a provably fair random selection process to determine winners based on ticket odds.

How It Works

Purchase tickets using ADA and await the draw's countdown completion. If your wallet is selected as a winner, prizes are automatically deposited into your wallet. No manual claiming required. Enjoy a seamless and transparent lottery experience powered by the Cardano blockchain.

Prize Distribution

Prizes are automatically distributed to winners' Cardano wallets immediately after each lottery round. The total prize pool is divided among three winners: 50% for first place, 30% for second place, and 20% for third place. To sustain the lottery dApp, a 2.5% commission is applied from the total pool, with an additional 2.5% allocated to a weekly jackpot, enhancing future rewards.

Transparency

All transactions and lottery results are securely recorded on the Cardano blockchain, ensuring complete transparency and verifiability. A comprehensive Lottery Winners history, including transaction IDs, is publicly displayed on our website. Our audited smart contract is openly accessible for review. Visit the Contact tab to access the contract files or reach out with any questions.
